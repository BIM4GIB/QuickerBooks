Claude × QuickBooks

Your books, in plain English. Ask Claude to pull reports, create invoices, look up customers, record payments — just by typing what you need. No menus. No spreadsheets. Just conversation.
What it looks like
"Run a P&L for Q1 2025"
Here's your Profit & Loss for Jan 1 – Mar 31, 2025. Revenue is up 12% vs last quarter, driven by consulting services...
"Create an invoice for Acme Corp — 10 hrs consulting at $150/hr, due net 30"
Done. Invoice #1042 created for Acme Corp, $1,500.00, due April 30. Want me to email it to them?

Get started in 5 minutes

Open Terminal on your Mac and paste:
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/BIM4GIB/QuickerBooks/main/install.sh | bash
1. Installs everything automatically
2. Walks you through connecting to QBO
3. Configures Claude Desktop for you
4. Restart Claude — you're live.
